B1 Evidence: Wrong-way entry in a reversible/HOV corridor (CBS Colorado, May 4, 2015)

• Aug. 31, 2013: head-on crash after wrong-way entry into the I-25/US-36 HOV segment.
• Reported outcome: 1 fatality and 1 critical injury.
• Report cites CDOT concern about ambiguous wrong-way entry paths and prior crashes.

Mapping to Scenario B1:
Lane-direction signal interpretation / lane-attribution error at reversible-lane entrances.
